# Resource Tracking Terminal

The **Resource Tracking Terminal** shows which resources are being consumed by the ME network, and at what rate.

It tracks items extracted by autocrafting and items pulled from the network into ME Interfaces.

---

## Overview grid

Items are displayed in a grid, similar to a standard ME Terminal.

The number shown on each item is its consumption rate in the last 60 seconds.

Items with no recent activity are removed from the grid automatically.

The search field filters by item name.

---

## Details view

Clicking an item opens a breakdown of where it was consumed.

Each row shows the amount and the source: a crafting job (with the output item icon) or an ME Interface at a specific coordinate.

Interface entries have an eye button. Clicking it highlights the interface block in the world with a red pulsing outline for 10 seconds.

The back button returns to the overview grid.
