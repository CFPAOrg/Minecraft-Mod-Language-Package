Prismarine Crystals glitter and glow in the deep, casting vibrant rainbow refractions over the ancient monument.


The crystalline structure of these shards, as formed deep underwater, 
seems to split any light passing through it into its component colors.

;;;;;

Glass can achieve a similar effect of course, if cut correctly, but not nearly to this extent.
It's almost as if these crystals amplify any light passing through.
And not just a little. They are easily bright enough to be used as lamps.


I have to wonder what is creating this strange phenomenon.
And more importantly, if I can harness it.
