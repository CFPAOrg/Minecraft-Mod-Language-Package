# Fat Collector

\columns[fit=second]{Fat collectors draw latent ethereal fats into their reservoirs.
}{\item_render{neepmeat:collector}}

## Usage

A Fat Collector must be placed with its arm facing a Rendering Basin. Provided enough Eldritch Enzymes are present in the basin, inserting any raw ore will produce its corresponding fat in the collector. Up to four Fat Collectors can be placed next to a single Rendering Basin to collect the fats from different ores.
