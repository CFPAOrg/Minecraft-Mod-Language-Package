# Smart Dumper

\columns[fit=second]{
The Smart Dumper drops items from its inventory directly below it. Items are only dropped if the inventory below can receive them. The internal inventory holds a single stack and items can be removed by right-clicking with an empty hand. 

Powering the dumper with redstone prevents it from dropping items.
}{\item_render{neepmeat:dumper}}
