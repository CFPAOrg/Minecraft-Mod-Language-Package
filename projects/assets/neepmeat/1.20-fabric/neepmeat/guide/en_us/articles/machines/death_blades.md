# Death Blades

The Death Blades are a regrettable instrument necessary for the acquisition of Tissue Slurry.

\item_render[height=30]{neepmeat:tissue_slurry_bucket}

## Usage

When supplied with a source of rotation, the entities in the path of the blades will be damaged. If an entity is killed above a drain, a small amount of Tissue Slurry will be collected.
