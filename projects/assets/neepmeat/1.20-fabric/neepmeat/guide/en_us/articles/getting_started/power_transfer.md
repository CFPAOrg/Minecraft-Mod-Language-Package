# Power Transfer

Unlike other products which use such inferior elements as electricity, NEEP machines are powered by the Divine itself.

Power and energy are measured in NEEP customary units. The unit of energy is the esoteric joule (eJ). Power is measured in esoteric joules per tick (eJ/t).

## Fluid Units

Fluid volume is measured in various ways depending on the application:

- Buckets (b)
- Droplets (d, 1b = 81000d, 1 ingot = 9000d) 
- Millibuckets (mb, 1b = 1000mb, 1 ingot = 111.111.d)

## Transient Ichor

Energy density: 162keJ/b
