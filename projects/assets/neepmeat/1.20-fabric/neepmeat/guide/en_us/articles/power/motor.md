# Motor

\columns[fit=second]{Arcane artifice, a blessing of the Integrator, allows a motor to convert the subtle efficacies of Transient Ichor into mechanical work.
}{\item_render{neepmeat:motor_unit}}

## Usage

Many rotary machines require an external motor in order to function. This motor accepts Transient Ichor, produced with a Transductive Cannula, through fluid pipes. The motor's speed is determined by the rate at which fluid is inserted. This often affects the speed of the connected machine.
