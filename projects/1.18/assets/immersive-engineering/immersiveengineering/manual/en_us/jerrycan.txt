Jerrycan
Better than buckets
<&recipe>Jerrycans are simple metal containers used to carry fluids. They can store up to 10 buckets and can pour that fluid into the world as well as into machines.
They cannot pick up fluids, however.<br>
Additionally, they can be used to refill fluid-consuming items like the <link;mining_drill;Mining Drill;start> by placing them together in a crafting grid.
