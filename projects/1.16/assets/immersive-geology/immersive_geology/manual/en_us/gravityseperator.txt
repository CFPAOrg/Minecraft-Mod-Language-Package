Gravity Separator
Rolling Stones
<&gravityseparator0>The Gravity Separator is a multiblock that is used to separate rock from ore by density. This is used to automatically turn dirty crushed ore into crushed ore.
Pump Water into the top of the machine, it will accept items dropped into it from the exposed sides, do not use a hopper or a direct interface.
The machine will not work without a source of water, items will be outputted at the bottom of the machine on either side.
