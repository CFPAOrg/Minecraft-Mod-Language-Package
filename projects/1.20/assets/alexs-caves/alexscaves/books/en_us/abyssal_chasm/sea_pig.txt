    Sea Pigs are gelatinous invertebrates found on the sea floor within the Abyssal Chasm. Slowly trudging along on their many legs, they sift through the Muck to look for anything edible.
    Sea Pigs can drop themselves if they die, and can be eaten, although their flesh is poisonous. They can be captured with a Bucket of Water.
