

    The piles of Guano that litter the floor of the Forlorn Hollows can be crafted into Fertilizer.






    Fertilizer works like bone meal, except it is much stronger. It can also be used to create Thornwood Saplings from branches.






    The layers of deposited Guanostone can also bear Redstone ore, making the Forlorn Hollows a source of redstone at higher depths than usual. Coprolith can also contain coal ore, too.
