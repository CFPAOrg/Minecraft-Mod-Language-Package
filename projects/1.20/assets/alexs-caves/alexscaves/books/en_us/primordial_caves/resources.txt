

    Limestone makes up a majority of the stone of this cave biome. It can be worked into spears that deal low ranged and melee damage, yet are stackable.






    Limestone can also be smelted into Smooth Limestone. If Charcoal is used on Smooth Limestone, a Cave Painting can be created.








    Of the two species of tree native to this biome, Pewens are the most common. They also produce Pine Nuts, which can be eaten. The Ancient Jungle Tree produces Tree Stars, which are a favorite food of many of this biome's prehistoric denizens...
