# PLC Workbenches

Workbenches allow items and entities to be modified by a PLC via an actuator.

## Examples:

- Display Plate (item)
- Pedestal (item)
- Surgery Platform (entity)
- 
Not all instructions require workbenches. Some operate on normal item or fluid inventories.
