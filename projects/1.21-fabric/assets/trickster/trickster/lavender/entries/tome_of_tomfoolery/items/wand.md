A wand is a very simple magical tool that will cast a spell inscribed into it when used with <keybind;key.use> or <keybind;key.attack>, giving {#aa3355}True{} or {#aa3355}False{} respectively as first argument.

<recipe;trickster:wand>

Spells can be inscribed into a wand using [Notulist's Ploy](^trickster:tricks/basic#4).
