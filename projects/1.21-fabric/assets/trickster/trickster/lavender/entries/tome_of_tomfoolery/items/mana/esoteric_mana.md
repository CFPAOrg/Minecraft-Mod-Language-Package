*"It is common knowledge that the moon provides mana to the world, and all that lives upon it."*


*"Its silver glow freely blankets our lands, ready to fuel our spellcasting."*


*"One might consider it strange then, that the most potent sources of mana be found where moonlight does not reach."*

;;;;;

-- An excerpt from a lecture by Prof. Olivine.

---

While mana can be obtained from the natural world in a myriad of ways, 
creating Esoteric Mana is arguably one of the easiest.

<recipe;trickster:esoteric_mana>

;;;;;

Simply combining squid ink from underwater cave systems with small shards of amethyst, 
both of which are known for their magical properties, will result in a mixture containing a very sizeable amount of mana.


This substance can be right-clicked onto a [Knot](^trickster:items/mana/knots) of any kind to refill it by roughly 512 Gandalfs.
